Tasty eggs recipe

$19.00

"For a delightful twist on traditional eggs, try making shakshuka, a flavorful Middle Eastern dish. Start by sautéing chopped onions, bell peppers, and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add diced tomatoes, cumin, paprika, and a pinch of chili powder, letting the mixture simmer until it thickens. Create small wells in the sauce and gently crack in eggs, covering the pan until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny. Finish with fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley and serve with crusty bread for dipping.

palak paneer

$19.99

To make palak paneer, start by blanching fresh spinach leaves in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then transfer them to an ice bath to retain the vibrant green color. Drain and blend the spinach into a smooth puree. In a pan, heat oil or ghee and sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until golden. Add cumin seeds, followed by the spinach puree, and cook for a few minutes with spices like garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Gently fold in cubed paneer and simmer until heated through. Finish with a splash of cream for richness, and serve hot with naan or rice for a deliciously creamy and nutritious dish!

paneer butter masala

$20.50

To make paneer butter masala, start by heating butter and oil in a pan, then sauté finely chopped onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant. Stir in chopped tomatoes, along with spices like red chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala, cooking until the tomatoes soften and the oil separates. Blend this mixture into a smooth sauce, then return it to the pan and add cream for richness. Gently fold in cubed paneer and simmer for a few minutes to absorb the flavors. Finish with a sprinkle of kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) and serve hot with naan or rice for a creamy, indulgent dish!
